About Me

Dr. Alla Myzelev is an Associate professor of Art History and Museum Studies at the State University of New York at Geneseo where she teaches courses in modern and contemporary visual culture. She has published extensively on feminism, activism, material culture and the DIY movement in North America. Myzelev is the author of Architecture, Design and Craft in Toronto 1900-1940: Creating Modern Living (Ashgate 2016). Her edited collection of essays Exhibiting Craft and Design: Transgressing the White Cube Paradigm has just been published in 2017. She is currently working on a manuscript on masculinity and politics of fashion in the Soviet Union.
